Evidence-first PJM DART research.

This stack is built to make signal research more repeatable and less vulnerable to accidental optimism. The work is not just about what the model says; it is about the conditions under which the result should be trusted at all.

Core pieces

The research flow includes a canonical pull pipeline, a baseline walk-forward runner, dataset and contract tests, and state-machine artifacts that keep phase transitions explicit instead of implied.

  • Ex-ante rules before holdout access.
  • Baseline walk-forward before later optimization claims.
  • Tests and artifacts that make the workflow traceable.
