Energy Analyst Agent
Back to projects

Real-time surveillance for PJM-MISO seam trading.

This system is built to surface the market state quickly enough to matter operationally. It tracks LMP, constraints, load, generation, and spreads, then routes alert context into Teams so the desk is not assembling the picture by hand under time pressure.

Configured scope

Around thirty streams are configured across PJM and MISO, with twenty-two alert rules covering LMP spikes, binding constraints, spread stress, load deviation, and data freshness.

  • PJM-MISO seam focus.
  • Streaming architecture for operational visibility.
  • Alert delivery through Microsoft Teams.
