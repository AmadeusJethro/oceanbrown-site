Agentic Ops Platform
Back to projects

Approval-routed execution for energy operations.

This system routes operational work through Microsoft Teams approval, then dispatches approved tasks to a Windows workcell VM for automated execution. The point is controlled execution with a human gate, a clean dispatch path, and an audit trail that survives after the work is done.

Why it exists

Operational tasks around a desk can become fragile quickly when they live in memory, chat messages, and one-off scripts. This platform turns them into something requestable, approvable, routable, and reviewable.

  • Teams approval before action.
  • Windows workcell execution where local applications and desk context matter.
  • Append-only audit trail for the full approval-to-dispatch path.
