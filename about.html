About

Power-market systems with a bias toward discipline and usability.

I work on the infrastructure around trading as much as the models themselves: how data gets pulled, how research gets validated, how alerts get delivered, and how recurring desk work stops depending on memory and cleanup effort.

The domain context is power markets across PJM, MISO, ERCOT, and NYISO. The systems range from research pipelines and monitoring stacks to Windows-side operational tooling that has to be useful in live workflows, not just technically interesting in isolation.

What I optimize for is lower friction, stronger evidence, clearer feedback loops, and systems that keep working after the first clean demo.
