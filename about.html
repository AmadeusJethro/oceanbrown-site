About

Real-time trading, power-market research, and AI-driven systems.

Currently, I work at Spotlight Power as a real-time trader, and I spend most of my time thinking about energy markets and artificial intelligence. I am most interested in building both fundamental and machine learning models for power markets, automating operational and trading workflows, and developing research systems for power-market analysis.

I am particularly interested in how AI can strengthen research and analysis in power markets. Previously, I interned at Cresta, an energy infrastructure fund.

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from the Georgia Institute of Technology and a B.A. in Philosophy from the University of California, Santa Barbara. Outside of work, I enjoy reading philosophy and training Brazilian jiu-jitsu and Muay Thai.
