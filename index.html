About

Currently, I work at Spotlight Power as a real-time trader. I spend most of my time thinking about power markets and artificial intelligence. I am most interested in conducting market analysis, building fundamental and machine learning models, and developing tools and automations to support that work. As it relates to AI, I am particularly interested in developing reliable agentic systems that can reason over large, complex datasets, support hypothesis generation and backtesting, and maintain verifiability and interpretability.

I hold an M.S. in Computer Science from the Georgia Institute of Technology and a B.A. in Philosophy from the University of California, Santa Barbara. Before joining Spotlight, I interned at Cresta Fund Management, an energy infrastructure private equity fund.

Outside of work, I enjoy reading philosophy and training Brazilian jiu-jitsu.
