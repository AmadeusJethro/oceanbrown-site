Section I

Market Design & Liquidity

On the microstructure of event markets and automated market maker parameterization

This project investigates the microstructure of prediction markets, comparing logarithmic market scoring rules against continuous limit order books under varying liquidity conditions. The central question concerns optimal parameterization of automated market makers to minimize adverse selection while preserving price discovery quality.

Empirical analysis of synchronized orderbook data from Polymarket and Kalshi reveals that cost-function market makers exhibit superior price stability in thin markets but incur higher inventory costs during rapid information arrival. A liquidity-sensitive fee structure adapts spreads dynamically based on realized volatility and order flow imbalance, achieving a 34% reduction in adverse selection costs.

  • MethodsSimulation, empirical orderbook analysis
  • Key FindingAdaptive LMSR −34% adverse selection
  • StatusActive research
Section II

Haven Selection & Shadow Trading

Systematic identification of persistent mispricings in event contract markets

Haven selection identifies prediction market contracts where prices persistently deviate from fair value due to structural factors. The shadow-trading system executes against these mispricings using a relative-value framework with positions sized by a modified Kelly criterion constrained by binary payoff structure.

Section III

Reliable Market Data Loggers

Fault-tolerant infrastructure for continuous market data capture

Research-quality prediction market analysis requires continuous, gap-free data capture across multiple exchanges. This project delivers fault-tolerant logging infrastructure with checkpoint-recovery architecture, automatic gap detection and backfill, and schema versioning for long-term data integrity.

Each logger maintains a write-ahead log of received messages, persists to Parquet with configurable partitioning, and registers heartbeats with a supervisor process. Current deployment covers Polymarket, Kalshi, and Metaculus with 99.94% uptime over 180 days of continuous operation.

  • Exchanges3 active (Polymarket, Kalshi, Metaculus)
  • Uptime99.94% / 180 days
