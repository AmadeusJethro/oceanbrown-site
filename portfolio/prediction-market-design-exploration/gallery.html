Selection Rationale

Given the academic tone, quantitative audience (trading/energy employers), and research-portfolio format, baseline picks favor restraint, typographic rigor, and institutional credibility. Originals fill gaps with LaTeX-manuscript, journal, mathematical, archival, and lecture-note aesthetics.

Baseline Picks (5 from B1–B10)

  • B4 Swiss Rational — Pure typographic hierarchy is the backbone of academic communication
  • B5 Editorial Longform — Publication layout with marginal notes maps to research writing
  • B7 Japanese Spatial — Extreme restraint signals intellectual confidence
  • B3 Sovereign Heraldic — Institutional formality with serif signals credibility
  • B6 Architectural Blueprint — Technical precision aligns with systems engineering

Project Originals (5 invented)

  • Academic Monograph — LaTeX/thesis layout with theorem blocks and definitions
  • Research Journal — Nature/Science two-column with structured abstract
  • Quantitative Notation — Mathematical manuscript with equation-style rhythm
  • Archive Index — Library catalog aesthetic with classification codes
  • Seminar Notes — Lecture notes with annotation margins and callouts

Detail.design Picks

  • Visibility Based Active Items in ToC — IntersectionObserver highlights current project in nav (applied in variations 01, 05)
  • Prevent Layout Shift from Font Weight Change — Fixed letter-spacing on nav active states to prevent reflow
  • Larger Hit Area than It Appears — Min 44px interactive targets via padding, even on minimal-looking elements
  • Natural Image Border — Stable visual anchors (hairlines, rules) at section boundaries across all variations
